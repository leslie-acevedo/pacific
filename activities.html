activities at pacific trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guet use.

Bird watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week.
